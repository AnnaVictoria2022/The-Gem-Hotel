Welcome to our boutique hotel in the heart of the Mediterranean

The Gem is conveniently situated on the picturesque coast in the heart of the world-renowned Santorini island

Just steps from the private beach, fine restaurants and hottest clubs of the region


Hotel amenities include:

  • Private beach
  • Infinity edge pool

  • Complimentary wi-fi in every room
  • Traditional greek restaurant on-site
